Welcome

I am currently a Miller Postdoctoral Fellow working with Ron Cohen and Inez Fung at the University of California at Berkeley. I work on a variety of topics related to atmospheric chemistry, remote sensing, and inverse modelling. Broadly, my research focuses on understanding how carbon cycles through the various reservoirs in the earth system (e.g., the atmosphere, ocean, and biosphere). My most recent work is on the decadal trends in atmospheric methane and hydroxyl, the main sink for methane and the primary oxidant in the troposphere.

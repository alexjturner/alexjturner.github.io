About me

I am currently a Miller Postdoctoral Fellow working with Ron Cohen and Inez Fung at the University of California at Berkeley. I completed my PhD in Atmospheric Chemistry in 2017 at Harvard University with Daniel Jacob and my undergraduate degree in Mechanical Engineering in 2012 at CU Boulder with Daven Henze.

I was born in Cambridge, MA and grew up in Boulder, CO. Growing up in Boulder helped foster a love of the environment and the outdoors. During the summer I spend my free time rock climbing, backpacking, and hiking. In the winter, I enjoy skiing and telemarking. The mountains have helped to shape who I am today and continue to fuel my interest in environmental sciences.
